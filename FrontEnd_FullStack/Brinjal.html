Indian Brinjal Curry

This delicious Indian Brinjal Curry is a flavorful dish made with fresh ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large brinjals (eggplants)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Wash and chop the brinjals into small pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add chopped brinjals and cook until they are tender.
  6. Beat yogurt and add it to the curry. Stir well.
  7. Add garam masala and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
